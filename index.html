The Artist

Meet Rikki

I'm Rikki, the artist behind Wink Wink. 25+ years in the industry, trained in cosmetology and lash artistry, honed at the top beauty salons in Beverly Hills, and mentored by some of the most respected names in the craft. My work has taken me on set, shaping looks for A-list celebrities on television. I invest in continuing education year after year, because every client deserves the very best of me.

Outside the studio, I'm a proud mom with an endless love for animals. Bookings are kept private so the studio stays calm, the work stays personal, and you always leave feeling like the best version of yourself.
